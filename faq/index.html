Questions & Guidelines

Everything you need to know about joining the Bangladesh National Cadet Corps.

Recruitment

Who can join BNCC?
Any student currently enrolled in Class 11 (First Year) or Honours 1st Year at Government Science College can apply. You must be physically fit and willing to dedicate time for training.

What are the physical requirements?
  • Height: Male: Minimum 5'6" | Female: Minimum 5'2"
  • Fitness: Must pass the physical endurance test (Running, Pushups).
  • Vision: Correctable vision is acceptable.

Benefits & Opportunities

Will BNCC help me join the Military?
Yes. BNCC cadets receive special preferences during ISSB and recruitment in Bangladesh Army, Navy, and Air Force. Specifically, you may bypass the written exam for the soldier recruitment process in some cases.

Do we get any academic marks?
Cadets who complete the course and pass the exams (Certificate B/C) are awarded additional marks in Honours/Degree final exams under National University.

Is there any chance to go abroad?
Absolutely. Selected cadets participate in Youth Exchange Programs (YEP) to countries like India, Maldives, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and Singapore as state ambassadors.

Training Routine

When are the parades held?
Regular parades are usually held twice a week (typically Tuesday and Friday) at the college premises from 02:00 PM to 05:00 PM.
