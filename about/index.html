The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) is a Para-Military force dedicated to serving the nation.

Our History

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) has a rich heritage. Its origins trace back to the University Officers Training Corps (UOTC) founded in 1927. After the liberation war of 1971, the need for a unified youth force was felt strongly.

On March 23, 1979, BNCC was officially formed by merging UOTC and the Junior Cadet Corps. Since then, it has been operating under the dual administration of the Ministry of Defence and the Ministry of Education.

"To develop the youth into disciplined and organized citizens, ready to serve the nation in times of natural calamities and war."
